Overview

My primary area of interest is plasma physics, turbulence and statistical mechanics. As a part of my DPhil studentship at Oxford, I am currently studying anomalous transport properties in weakly collisional plasma with a particular interest in anomalous diffusion due to micro-instabilities under the supervision of Dr. Archie Bott and Prof. Alex Schekochihin. My work contains both analytical studies and numerical experiments, particularly particle in cell simulation.

During my undergraduate years, I have worked in turbulence, statistical mechanics and magneto-hydrodynamics particularly nature of turbulent flow and its statistics, non-equilibrium phase separation, criticality and marginality and decaying MHD turbulence.
